Clavus

Sync Ableton projects across machines — no cloud, no subscription.
Your always-on relay. Your samples. Your music.

🔄

Real-time Sync

Push and pull snapshots across machines via a personal Tailscale relay. No uploading to a third party.

🧬

Content-Addressed

Samples and audio are deduplicated globally. Same sample on both machines? Stored once, referenced everywhere.

📸

Snapshot & Restore

Save checkpoints before big changes. Restore any previous snapshot instantly. Diff two versions side by side.

🤝

Collaborate

Share your relay URL with a collaborator. They join, pull your project, push changes back. Live collaboration.

Clavus is free and open source. If it's useful to you, support is appreciated.
